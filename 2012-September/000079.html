Dear Friend, I publish today the beta 4 of ledmac 1 (before we choice the final name).

- ledarab is definitivly unmaintained. 
- debug in familiar footnote and end footnote with command inside.
- 6 news hooking command :
	- For the font family of number in notes (command must be a switching command, like \itshape):
		- Xnotenumfont[series]{command}
		- Xendnotenumfont[series]{command}
		- notenumfontX[series]{command} 
	- For the font size of notes (command must be a size command, like \small)
		- \Xnotefontsize[series]{command}
		- \Xendnotefontsize[series]{command}
		- \notefontsizeX[series]{command}

Beta could be downloaded in https://github.com/maieul/ledmac/zipball/mac1.0%CE%B24-par1.0%CE%B24
