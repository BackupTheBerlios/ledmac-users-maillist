Dear friends,

I have just published on ctan ledmac 0.19 and ledpar 0.14. These versions come back to version 0.17 and 0.13. It's normally, the last version of ledmac / ledpar (ledarab).

I also publish the sucessor of ledmac and ledpar, which is eledmac/eledpar (e for extended).

The most important change is addition of a lot of commands to customize notes display (I invite you to read the manual).

An other news is the add of \newseries command, to generate a new series of notes (end, familiar and critical), without no other thing to do.

In code, I used the package etoolbox and xargs, to have a more easy maintenance. A little consequence of this use of etoolbox is that the max room grow up from 256 to 65536. That is why I set the default maxchunks of eledpar to 5120 (proportional change), but I think people could grow up more, because I don't think that 55296 box rooms are used by other packages.

To migrate from ledmac to eledmac, you should read Appendix A of eledmac documentation. The changes have to be made if you made customization described in the ledmac documentation, by overload fmt commands (and by duplicating Xfootnote commands).

Thank you to all people who tested, and to Michael for english correction in doc.
