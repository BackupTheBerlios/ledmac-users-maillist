Fooling about with past versions of ledmac, I find that ledmac 2011/11/18
= v0.13.1 (github version mac0.13.1-par0.9.3-arab0.1) is the most recent
one that compiles my current working text edition without "missing number"
errors.

The format of my edition was set up some years back, and I've been
concentrating only on the philological content since then.
