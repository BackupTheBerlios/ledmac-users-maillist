You can use \raisebox. In your example:

...
\begin{center}
\edt{Letter 1}{\A{blah}}
\linebreak\linebreak
To the \edt{Countess of ------}{\B{blahblahblah}}\raisebox{-1ex}{.}
%Probably way too low, but it will give you the idea and you can
adjust the value of ex until it looks right to you
\linebreak\linebreak\linebreak
\end{center}
...
