Dear friends,

I posted on the CTAN a new release of ledmac (0.12.1) and ledpar (0.9.1)

  For ledpar, fix a bug who made impossible to use \autopar on the right side.

For ledmac and pstart, propose to insert a number on every pstart command. Use \numberpstarttrue to enable it and \numberpstartfalse to disable it.

To change the apparence of the numbering, redefine the command \thepstart, \thepstartL and \thepstartR.

To reset the numbering, make :

\setcounter{pstart}{1}

and if you use ledpar

\setcounter{pstartL}{1}
\setcounter{pstartR}{1}

The release will be public when the servor of the CTAN will be up.
