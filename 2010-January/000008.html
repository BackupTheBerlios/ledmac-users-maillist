the problem lies within your use of \SingleSpacing and \DoubleSpacing.
When I set the \baselineskip manually instead, everything seems to
work.  When you change these lines to \baselineskip=10pt
resp. \baselineskip=20pt everything works.

Personally, I don't use the memoir class.  You should perhaps ask on
their mailing list (if their is any) or in comp.text.tex.
