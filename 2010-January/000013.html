> This should fit your needs, although it's a little hackish and not very
> tidy.
> 
> \renewcommand*{\twocolfootfmt}[3]{%
>   \baselineskip=10pt
> \normal at pars
> \hsize .3\hsize
> \setlength{\parindent}{0pt}
> \tolerance=5000
> \raggedright
> \hangindent2.5em \hangafter1
> \leavevmode
> \strut\hbox to 1.3em{\notenumfont\printlines#1|\hfil}\ignorespaces
> {\select at lemmafont#1|#2}\rbracket\enskip
> #3\strut\par\allowbreak
> \vspace{5pt}% added
> }
> 
> \newcommand{\calibreak}{\\\hangindent2.5em \hangafter1 % changed
> \hspace{1.3em}} % Defines the CA line break indent
