Hi all,

I've just found out that LaTeX's \markboth mechanism breaks ledmac's output when used within a paragraph.

\documentclass{article}
\usepackage{eledmac}
\begin{document}
\beginnumbering
\pstart
Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consetetur sadipscing elitr, \markboth{1}{2} sed diam nonumy eirmod tempor invidunt ut labore et dolore magna aliquyam erat, sed diam voluptua.  
\pend
\endnumbering
\end{document}

I use a custom pagestyle to print the chapter/verse range for each page into the header (e.g. 1,6--1,12), which makes it necessary to call \markboth several times per paragraph. Is there another way to get this working?
